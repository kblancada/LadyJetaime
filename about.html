Bringing A Fantastical Theatrical World About Lady Jetaime

A creativity born in the beautiful theater of Kurosawa, the epic writing of Homer, and the whimsical style of the Disney Renaissance. My dream is to bring forth substance and style in an entertaining and worthwhile visual medium that'll leave your jaw dropped and your world changed.

Classically trained in the styles of George Bridgman, Frank Reilly, and Richard Williams. My interest is specifically in the beauty of movement and life. Combined with a decade's worth of experimentation into achieving 'Digital Painterly', you won't find another artist with my creative sensibilities.
